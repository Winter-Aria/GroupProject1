Network Engineer

Reference Number: BA113

Position Title: Network Engineer

We are looking for a passionate Network Engineer to join our growing team.

You'll be responsible for the design, implementation, and maintenance of our network infrastructure, ensuring seamless connectivity and optimal performance.

Salary Range: 100,000-120,000

Reports To: Manager

Key Responsibilities

  • Design and implement network solutions to meet evolving business needs.
  • Troubleshoot and resolve network issues to minimize downtime and ensure smooth operations.
  • Configure and manage network devices, including routers, switches, firewalls, and VPNs.
  • Monitor network performance and implement improvements to optimize efficiency.

Qualifications

Essential

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Information Technology, Network Engineering, or a related field.
  2. Cisco Certified Network Associate (CCNA) or equivalent certification (e.g., CompTIA Network+).

Preferable

  1. Advanced Networking Certifications such as:
    • Cisco Certified Network Professional (CCNP)
    • Certified Information Systems Security Professional (CISSP)
    • Juniper Networks Certified Associate (JNCIA)
    • AWS Certified Advanced Networking (for cloud networking roles)
  2. 2 years of practical experience in large scale networking

Software Developer

Reference Number: AB232

Position Title: Software Developer

We are looking for a dedicated Software Developer to join our dynamic team.

You will be responsible for designing, developing, and maintaining software applications, ensuring high performance, scalability, and reliability.

Salary Range: 90,000-110,000

Reports To: Lead Software Engineer

Key Responsibilities

  • Develop, test, and maintain software applications following best practices.
  • Collaborate with cross-functional teams to define, design, and implement new features.
  • Debug and resolve technical issues to improve application performance.
  • Write clean, maintainable, and efficient code following industry standards.
  • Stay updated with emerging technologies to enhance development processes.

Qualifications

Essential

  1. Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Software Engineering, or a related field.
  2. Proficiency in at least one programming language (e.g., Python, Java, C#, JavaScript).

Preferable

  1. Experience with frameworks and tools such as:
    • React, Angular, or Vue.js (for frontend development)
    • .NET, Spring Boot, or Django (for backend development)
    • SQL and NoSQL databases (e.g., PostgreSQL, MongoDB)
  2. 2+ years of hands-on experience in software development.
  3. Knowledge of cloud platforms like AWS, Azure, or Google Cloud.
  4. Familiarity with DevOps practices and CI/CD pipelines.

IT Support Specialist

Reference Number: D1337

Position Title: IT Support Specialist

We are looking for a dedicated IT Support Specialist to provide technical assistance to clients as well as company administration and staff

Salary Range: 70,000-90,000

Reports To: IT Manager

Key Responsibilities

  • Provide technical support for desktops, laptops, and mobile devices.
  • Install and configure software, operating systems, and peripherals.
  • Troubleshoot network connectivity and system performance issues.

Preferable

Human Resources Coordinator

Reference Number: HE451

Position Title: Human Resources Coordinator

We are seeking a Human Resources Coordinator to support our HR operations, ensuring a smooth experience for employees and candidates.

Salary Range: 75,000-95,000

Reports To:HR Manager

Key Responsibilities

  • Assist in recruitment efforts, including job postings, resume screening, and interview coordination.
  • Facilitate onboarding and training programs for new hires.

  3. 1-2 years of experience in HR or a related administrative role.
